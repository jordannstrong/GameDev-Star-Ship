How To Play:

Avoid asteroids and enemy fire while you fight your way out of the asteroid belt. Collect weapon upgrades to defeat the boss and escape!

W - Move up

A - Move left

S - Move down

D - Move right

Left click - Shoot primary weapon

Right click - Shoot railgun
